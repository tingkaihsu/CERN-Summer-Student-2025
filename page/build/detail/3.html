Ge76 Basic Properties

Q value: 6.071 [MeV]

MACS (n_TOF) @ 30 keV: 19.2 ± 0.9 [mb]

Main Publication of the n_TOF Collaboration:
Measurement of the 76Ge(n,γ) cross section at the n_TOF facility at CERN
Gawlik-Ramięga, A., Lederer-Woods, C., Krticcka, M., Valenta, S., Battino, U., Andrzejewski, J., Perkowski, J., Aberle, O., Audouin, L., Bacak, M. et al. (The n_TOF Collaboration)
Physical Review C 104, 044610 (2021)

Comment:
To determine the MACS including all relevant neutron energies, we have combined our data with theoretical predictions of the cross section from 52 keV to 1 MeV. We present MACS values using TALYS 1.9 with default parametrization, MACS values at 5 and 10 keV are almost entirely determined by the experimental data and TALYS predictions of MACS for kT < 10 keV agree the best (within 7%) with experimental data. The uncertainty on the total MACS has been estimated using the experimental uncertainty of the low-energy (En < 52 keV) component and an assumed uncertainty of 10% on the TALYS additional component.
